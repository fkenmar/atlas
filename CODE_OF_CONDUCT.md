# Code of conduct

atlas should be a useful, low-drama place for people improving developer tools.
Be direct, be kind, and keep criticism aimed at the work.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Assume good intent until there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Give actionable feedback with examples when possible.
- Respect different experience levels and tool preferences.
- Accept maintainer decisions about scope, timing, and tradeoffs.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or attention.
- Insults based on identity, background, or ability.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly pushing off-scope demands after a maintainer decision.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or
limit participation when behavior harms the project. Enforcement should be
proportionate, documented when practical, and focused on restoring a productive
project space.

If you see a problem, contact the maintainer through GitHub. Include links,
screenshots, or context when available.
